Total Fees

Admission Fees + 8 Semester Fees + 48 Monthly Fees = 9,000 Tk. + (6,000 Tk. × 8 semesters) + (1,500 Tk. × 48 months)

= 9,000 Tk. + 48,000 Tk. + 72,000 Tk.

Total Fees = 1,29,000 Tk.
