Inside of Computer Technology

Computer engineers develop, design, test and maintain computer hardware and software. Read on to learn more about degree and career options in the field of computer engineering

Majors

Computer engineers create and maintain computer systems and may specialize in one aspect of computer engineering, such as hardware or software engineering. Hardware engineers design computer circuitry, disks, chips, printers and other computer devices, while computer software engineers develop computer applications and programs. Good math and analytical skills are necessary for students interested in a career in computer engineering. If you are thinking about becoming a computer engineer, CPI Polytechnic has information on education and career paths in the field.
